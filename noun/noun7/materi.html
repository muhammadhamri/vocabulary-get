PENGGUNAAN OCUPATION KEDALAM KALIMAT

1.KALIMAT POSITIVE (PERNYATAAN)

Subject + To be + Ocupation

She is a Teacher.

He is a Chef.

He is a Driver.

She is a House Wife.

He is a Mechanic.

He is a Pilot.

2.KALIMAT NEGATIVE (MENIDAKKAN)

Subject + To be not + Ocupation

My mother is not a midwife
but she is a teacher.

He is not a Singer
but He is a chef.

He is not a farmer
but he is a driver.

She is not a teacher
but she is a House Wife.

He is not a carpenter
but he is a Mechanic.

He is not lawyer
but he is a Pilot.

1.KALIMAT INTROGATIVE (PERTANYAAN)

Tobe + Subject + Ocupation?

Are they farmer?

Is he a Carpenter?.

Is he a Doctor?.

Is she a Singer?

Are they fisherman?

Is he a Lawyer?
