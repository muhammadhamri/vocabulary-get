there is dan There are (ada)

There is dan there are adalah phrase yang digunakan untuk menyatakan keberadaan suatu benda. There is digunakan untuk menyatakan keberadaan benda yang jumlahnya satu (tunggal) sedangkan there are digunakan untuk menyatakan keberadaan benda yang jumlahnya lebih dari satu (jamak)
Contoh kalimat nya dalah:

Kalimat Positive (pernyataan)

- There is a cat in the box (Ada seekor kucing didalam kotak)
- There is an elephant in the stable. (Ada seekor gajah didlam kandang)
- There is an eagle on the sky (Ada seekor elang di angkasa)

- There are two rabbit in the cage. (Ada dua kelinci di dalam kandang)
- There are some ants in the ground (Ada beberapa semut di atas tanah)
- There are five birds on the branch (Ada lima ekor burung di atas dahan)

Kalimat Negative (Menyangkal)

-Is there a dog in the yard? (Apakah ada seekor anjing di halaman?)
-Is there a cat under the chair? (Apakah seekor kucing di bawah kursi?)
-Is there snake in the bush? (apakah ada ular di semak-semak itu?)

-Are there some ants on the ground? ( Apakah ada banyak semut di tanah?)
-Are there many crocodile in the zoo? (apakah ada banyak buaya di kebun binatang?)
- Are there two birds in the cage? (Apakah ada dua ekor burung di dalam sangkar?)

Kalimat Negative (Menyangkal)

-There is not a monkey on the tree (Tidak ada monyet di atas pohon itu)
-There is not a bird on branch (Tidak ada burung di atas dahan)
-There is not an elephant in the stable (Tidak ada gajah di dalam kandanag)

Penggunaan A dan An

A dan An berarti sebuah,seorang atau seekor

A digunakan untuk benda yang diawali oleh huruf consonant seperti:

- A Book : sebuah buku
- A Table : sebuah meja
- A Chair : sebuah kursi
- A Pencil : sebuah pensil
- A Door : sebuah pintu
- A Window : sebuah jendela
- A tiger : seekor harimau
- A cat : seekor kucing
- A doctor : seorang dokter
- A policeman : seorang polisi

AN digunakan untuk benda yang diawali oleh huruf Vocal seperti:

- An eagle : seekor elang
- An elephant : seekor gajah
- An ant : seekor semut
- An artist : seorang artis
- An enginer : seorang insinyur
- An apple : sebuah apel
- An eraser : Sebuah penghapus
- An orange : sebuah jeruk

Elephants: The Gentle Giants

Elephants are the biggest animals that live on land. They are known for their huge size, long trunks, and big ears. There are two main types of elephants: African elephants and Asian elephants. African elephants are bigger and have larger ears compared to Asian elephants.

An elephant's trunk is very special. It is a long nose that they use for many things. Elephants use their trunks to pick up food, drink water, and even give themselves a shower! They can pick up small things like a piece of fruit or big things like tree branches.

Elephants also have big ears, especially African elephants. These ears help them stay cool in hot weather. They flap their ears like fans to cool down. Elephants can also show their feelings by moving their ears.Elephants live in family groups called herds. The leader of the herd is usually the oldest female elephant, called the matriarch. She helps guide the group and teaches the young elephants. Male elephants leave the herd when they grow up and often live alone or with other males.

Elephants are very smart animals. They have great memories and can remember places and other elephants for many years. They can also show feelings like happiness, sadness, and love. Sometimes, they even cry when they are sad.Sadly, elephants face problems because people cut down trees where they live and hunt them for their tusks. It's important to protect elephants so they can live safely in the wild.In summary, elephants are amazing animals with big bodies, long trunks, and smart brains. They live in families and care for each other. We need to help protect these gentle giants so they can live happily in their homes.
