RECOUNT TEXT

Apa itu Recount Text?

Recount text adalah jenis teks yang menceritakan kembali pengalaman atau kejadian yang telah terjadi di masa lalu. Tujuan utama dari recount text adalah untuk memberikan informasi atau menghibur pembaca dengan cara menceritakan pengalaman atau peristiwa yang dialami oleh penulis atau orang lain. Recount text biasanya ditulis dalam bentuk naratif dan menggunakan past tense (masa lampau).

Ciri-Ciri Recount Text:

1.Tujuan: Untuk menginformasi atau menghibur pembaca atas peristiwa yang dialami atau dilihat pada masa lampau..

2.Struktur:
- Orientation: Bagian ini memperkenalkan latar belakang cerita, seperti siapa, di mana, dan kapan kejadian terjadi
- Events: Bagian ini menceritakan rangkaian peristiwa atau kejadian yang terjadi secara kronologis.
- Re-orientation: Bagian ini biasanya menyimpulkan cerita atau memberikan komentar pribadi penulis tentang kejadian tersebut.

1.Menggunakan kata hubung (conjunctions) dan kata keterangan waktu (time connectives) untuk menyusun urutan peristiwa

3.Bahasa: Menggunakan deskripsi untuk memberikan detail tentang kejadian atau pengalaman..

4.Pola kalimat yang digunakan adalah Simple Past tense. Untuk memahami simple Past tense silahkan klik Simple Past Tense

Contoh Recount Text

My First Trip to Bali

Oreientation :
Last year, during the summer holidays, I had the opportunity to visit Bali for the first time with my family. We were very excited about the trip because we had heard so much about the beautiful beaches and cultural attractions in Bali.
Events :
On the first day, we arrived at Ngurah Rai International Airport in the afternoon. After checking into our hotel, we decided to explore Kuta Beach. The beach was crowded with tourists, but the view of the sunset was absolutely breathtaking. We took many photos and enjoyed the cool sea breeze. The next day, we visited Ubud, a place known for its arts and crafts. We visited the Ubud Monkey Forest and had a lot of fun watching the playful monkeys. We also visited several art galleries and bought some beautiful paintings and handicrafts as souvenirs. On the third day, we went to Tanah Lot, a famous temple located on a rock formation in the sea. The sight of the temple against the backdrop of the ocean was truly mesmerizing. In the evening, we watched a traditional Kecak dance performance, which was a fascinating experience.
Re-orientation
Our trip to Bali was an unforgettable experience. We not only enjoyed the natural beauty of the island but also learned a lot about Balinese culture and traditions. I hope to visit Bali again someday to explore more of its hidden treasures.
